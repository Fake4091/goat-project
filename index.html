My GOAT

Who They Are

The GOAT of singing and making music in my opinion has to be Ado. She is a japanese singer who is known for their impressive vocal performances, both in the vocal booth, and even more so on stage. She debuted in 2020 at only 17 years old with her single "Usseewa," and has been releasing new songs and some covers frequently ever since. She has kept her face hidden during performances, only showing her silhouette. Pictured to the right is her character, which is used in promotional art as well as most of her album, EP, and single cover art.

Accomplishments
"Usseewa" #1 peak spots: Billboard Japan Hot 100 (later year chart pictured right), the Oricon Digital Singles Chart, and the Oricon Streaming Chart
Most Notable Achievements to the Average Person: Wrote the theme song for One Piece Film: Red titled "New Genesis" (Pictured left)
Most Notable Billboard Placement: 95 on Apple Music Global Top 100 of for "New Genesis"
